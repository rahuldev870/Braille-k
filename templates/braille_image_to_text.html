How It Works

This tool analyzes images containing Braille patterns and converts them to readable text. Follow these steps:

  1. Upload an image containing Braille dot patterns.
  2. Click "Process Braille Image" to analyze the image.
  3. The system will detect Braille dots, identify the characters, and convert them to text.
  4. You can click "Read Aloud" to hear the detected text spoken.
For best results, use well-lit images with good contrast between the Braille dots and background.
